Guess The Number: The Game

Rules and Instruction

  • You have only 5 guesses to guess a random number.
  • The number will always be a multiple of 5 between 5 and 100.
  • You can ask for a hint that'll tell you whether or not the random number is greater than 50. But it'll cost you two guesses!
  • Press the "reset" button to play another round.
  • You get an extra point if you win without using a hint!
